Egg Drop Soup


A great alternative to chicken noodle! Goes best with asian dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • Green Onions or chives, to taste
  • Optional: carrots, peas, sprouts

Directions:

  1. Bring broth to a simmer
  2. Add optional vegetables
  3. Scramble eggs in a separate bowl
  4. Slowly pour in the eggs in the simmering broth while stirring soup
  5. Salt & Pepper to taste
  6. Top with green onions or chives
