Broth based soup with eggs and vegetables

Egg drop soup is a great alternative to chicken noodle soup! It goes best with asian dishes. Egg drop soup is a healthy choice to start a family dinner. If the weather if cold, or if you are feeling under the weather, it's a simple and delicious soup to choose.

The simplicity of this recipe is amazing. With only a few simple ingredients, you can have yourself a bowl of healthy and delicious soup. Don't be afraid to try it out! Simpliest recipe is below:

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • Green Onions or chives, to taste
  • Optional: carrots, peas, sprouts

Steps:

  1. Bring broth to a simmer
  2. Add optional vegetables
  3. Scramble eggs in a separate bowl
  4. Slowly pour in the eggs in the simmering broth while stirring soup
  5. Salt & Pepper to taste
  6. Top with green onions or chives
